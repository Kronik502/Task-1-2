My journey begins in the vibrant streets of Seshego, South Africa, where I was born and raised. From a young age, music has been my constant companion, weaving its melodies into the fabric of my existence. Growing up in a community where music is more than just a form of entertainment, it's a way of life, I was exposed to a diverse range of rhythms and beats that ignited a passion within me.

As a child, I would spend hours mesmerized by the sounds emanating from the local shebeens, the rhythmic thumping of drums echoing through the night, and the soulful melodies of jazz filling the air. It was here that I discovered the power of music to transcend barriers and unite people from all walks of life.

Alongside my love for music, I developed a keen interest in technology, particularly computers. In a society where access to resources is often limited, computers played a pivotal role in empowering the black community, providing opportunities for education, innovation, and economic advancement.

With each keystroke, I delved deeper into the world of technology, honing my skills and expanding my knowledge. I realized that just as music has the power to uplift and inspire, so too does technology have the power to transform lives and create positive change.

Today, as I navigate through life's journey, I remain steadfast in my commitment to harnessing the power of music and technology to make a difference in my community and beyond. Whether through creating soul-stirring melodies or developing innovative solutions, I strive to leave a lasting impact and inspire others to follow their passions.

This is who I am – a dreamer, a creator, and a believer in the endless possibilities that lie ahead. Join me as we embark on this journey together, one beat, one byte at a time.
