In the bustling streets of Tembisa, South Africa, I took my first breath, surrounded by the rich tapestry of music that colored our community's existence. From the rhythmic beats of local shebeens to the soulful tunes of jazz, music was the heartbeat of our neighborhood.

Growing up, I found myself drawn not only to the melodies but also to the world of technology. Despite limited resources, I seized every opportunity to explore the realm of computers, fascinated by their potential to empower and uplift our community.

During my schooling years at Northern Academy, a private institution, I delved into the realms of Delphi programming, crafting simple yet meaningful programs like calculators. Transitioning to Ekurhuleni West College for Grade 12 due to financial constraints, I immersed myself in Visual Basic, mastering its syntax while developing practical applications like menu systems for local eateries.

Balancing my passion for technology with real-world responsibilities, I took on the role of manager at Lubo Cafe, overseeing daily operations with diligence and dedication. Despite the demands of work, I remained focused on my studies, eventually landing a position at Vikela Logistics to sustain myself financially.

Although my professional journey has yet to fully embrace the realm of computer technology, my hunger for learning remains insatiable. Armed with a voracious appetite for knowledge and a knack for quick adaptation, I am poised to seize any opportunity that comes my way in the ever-evolving world of technology.

As I continue to tread this path, fueled by my unwavering belief in the transformative power of music and technology, I stand ready to make a difference in my community and beyond. With each beat and byte, I am determined to leave an indelible mark on the world, one step at a time.
